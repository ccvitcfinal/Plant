What is a plant?

Here we try to answer some of the burning questions such as "what is a plant?", "How is a plant?", "When is a plant", "Where is a plant?", etc...

This is called a plant (belonging to the kingdom Plantae). They are generally green in colour. When birds poop, they poop out seeds and plants grow from the said seeds. This is generally done by storks. As a result, according to many scientists around the world, birds are considered half-parents of plants too.



How to grow a plant?

The bottom half of the object is called the “pot”. The pot contains soil. This soil contains nutrients for the plant to eat. They also prefer sunlight but this particular plant is an indoor plant so it is not mandatory to place in direct sunlight. But after eating, plants want to drink water. Most people assume, they drink water through the leaves so they tried to feed the plan using a syringe. But please don’t! Our warranty will not cover any damage done via this sacrilegious task. Instead, we highly recommend that the water be poured in the soil. If there is excess, fret not. The plate at the bottom collects the drained water. Hence, you can recycle water also. In today’s world where clean water is super rare, our efficient system can provide a practical and yet simple solution to conserve water (Japan karan edho edho kandupudichiruka, Naa edha kandupudichiruka paru).
